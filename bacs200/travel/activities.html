Swimming at the Beach

You can swim at the beach as long as you want before dark.You can build sand castles, or just lay in the sun. At the beach there also vendors so you can buy items to remeber your stay.

Watch Performances

There are performances shown around town. You can see many different types of dancing, bands and singers, and also impressionist of famouse singers.

Paragliding

Want to get a better view of the ocean, there are vendors who offer paragliding rides that save you a lot of money that will make you forget about heights.

Go to Clubs

Wanna have fun during the night as well, there are many different types of clubs you can go to. You can go drink, dance, or gamble.Clubs are open for most of the night so you can go club hopping if you dont want the day to end
